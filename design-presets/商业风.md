# 商业风 / Business / Corporate

## Palette

- Background: clean dark `#0e1118` to `#161922` gradient
- Primary text: `#f0f3f7` near-white
- Brand accent: `#3aa6ff` confident blue (NOT gold — that's for premium consumer)
- Secondary: `#7e94b3` muted steel
- Success: `#3dd078`. Caution: `#f4a13a`. Negative: `#e0524b`.

## Typography

- Sans (default): system / Inter / "PingFang SC"
- Display: weight 600, letter-spacing 0.02em — assertive but not loud
- Mono: tabular-nums on every number

## Motion

- Eases: `power2.out`, `expo.out` for entries; `sine.inOut` for ambient
- Stagger: 60-100 ms — tighter, more decisive
- Camera bias: **medium** — confident moves, not gentle
- No bounce/back eases on entrances (feels childish in business context)

## Method preference

- Pricing / metrics → `rm-d3-bar-chart` or `rm-framer-card-stack`
- Feature lists → `hf-anime-scatter`
- Transitions: prefer `cut` and `wipe-left` (decisive, not poetic)
- Hero claims → `hf-kinetic-text` with strong color (no gold gradient — solid blue)

## Voice

- Default voice: `male-qn-jingying` (精英男 — crisp business)
- Cinematic punchlines → `audiobook_male_1`
- Use `presenter_female` for "the customer said …" style quotes
- Total: 2 voices is enough. Don't get cute.

## Don'ts

- No purple/gold (that's premium-consumer, not business)
- No `kenburns` motion on data charts
- No `dip-to-black` mid-pitch — kills momentum
