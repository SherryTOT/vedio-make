# 情感叙事 / Emotional Narrative

## Palette

- Background: warm dark `#0f0a14` to `#08060c` gradient
- Primary text: `#fff4e0` warm cream
- Accent: `#e89f6a` sunset orange + `#7a5fbf` dusky purple
- Lowlight: heavy vignettes, dim corners

## Typography

- Serif option preferred for hero: "Noto Serif SC" / display weight
- Sans for narration: "PingFang SC", regular weight
- Letter-spacing: 0.06em on display (breathing room)
- Line-height: 1.5+ on body (slow read)

## Motion

- Eases: `sine.inOut`, `power2.inOut` only — gentle, organic
- AVOID `back.out` / `expo.out` — too snappy for this mood
- Stagger: 150-220 ms — leisurely
- Camera bias: **strong** kenburns-in on portraits; long holds; lots of dip-to-black

## Method preference

- Hero phrases → `hf-kinetic-text` with serif weight, NO bounce
- Portrait shots → strong kenburns + spotlight focus on face
- Lists / data → DEMOTE — this style isn't about facts
- Transitions: heavy `dip-to-black` for chapter changes, `fade` for soft pivots

## Voice

- Default: `audiobook_male_1` (deep, contemplative) OR `audiobook_female_1`
- Pull-quotes → `female-chengshu` (mature, warm) OR `male-qn-qingse` (青年 — younger)
- Voice change marks chapter / time shift in the story
- Aim for 3-5 voice changes per 90 s

## Don'ts

- No bright/saturated colors
- No snappy entrances (back.out)
- No tight stagger (60ms+ feels like punching the viewer)
- No data charts (rm-d3-*) — they break the spell
